1.Ice Holds Evidence of Ancient, Massive Solar Storm

An analysis of radioactive chemicals in ice cores indicates one of the most powerful solar storms ever hit Earth around 7176 B.C.The event created distinctive radioactive varieties of beryllium and chlorine in the atmosphere; those isotopes then fell to the ground with the yearly seasonal snowfall and were preserved in layers of ancient ice.    https://www.insidescience.org/index.php/news/ice-holds-evidence-ancient-massive-solar-storm
